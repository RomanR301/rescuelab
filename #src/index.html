Our mission

The Rescue lab

We are committed to saving coral reefs around the world. This online space is an invitation to take action. We believe that by empowering local communities we can ensure the survival of coral reefs in the future.

About the lab

This is an online community to support locals in their efforts to save coral reefs. We collect these efforts in an archive to inspire and build upon each others projects.
